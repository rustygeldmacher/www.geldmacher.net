### Route

  * Hell Brook Trail to Long Trail - 2.0 miles
  * Long Trail to Mount Mansfield summit - 0.3 miles
  * Long Trail from Mount Mansfield summit to parking area - 3.3 miles

**Total distance: 5.6 miles**
