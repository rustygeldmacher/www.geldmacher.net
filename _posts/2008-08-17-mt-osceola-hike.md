### Route

  * Mt. Osceola Trail from Tripoli Road to summit of Mt. Osceola - 3.2 miles
  * Mt. Osceola Trail from summit of Mt. Osceola to Tripoli Road - 3.2 miles

**Total distance: 6.4 miles**
